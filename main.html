Welcome to Xentaria

The world of Xentaria is a strange place full of mysteries, adventures and stories to be told. It is a self-made world set in the Dungeons and Dragons multiverse. This website will give you a short impression on the historical events that shaped the world, the different gods that influence it, the races that inhabit it, as well as the regions and political parties that are relevant today. Feel free to explore, learn about the universe and find a race that fits your character needs if you want to create one. But beware! In Xentaria it isn't as easy to revive someone as it might be in other D&D universes. So choose wisely.
Further down you can see the world map of Xentaria. The most important and well know places are shown here. Come back to see more regional maps in the future.
