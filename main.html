Welcome to the World of Xentaria

The world of Xentaria is a strange place full of mysteries, adventures and other things to be discovered. This webside will give you a short impression on the Timeline that shaped the world, the different gods that influence it, the races that inhabit it, as well as the regions and political parties that are relevant today. Feel free to explore and find a race that fits your character needs. To your right you can see the world map of Xentaria. The most important and well know places are shown here. Come back to see more regional maps in the future.
